Chapel Hill, NC Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Chapel Hill is a town in Orange and Durham counties in the U.S. state of North Carolina. Its population was 61,960 in the 2020 census, making Chapel Hill the 17th-largest municipality in the state. Chapel Hill, Durham, and the state capital, Raleigh, make up the corners of the Research Triangle (officially the Raleigh–Durham–Cary, NC Combined Statistical Area), with a total population of 2,106,463 in 2020 census.
